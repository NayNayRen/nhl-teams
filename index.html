Built By :

My name is Nate, I'm a Junior Web Developer of a little more than two years. I thought I would build this app out as a meens of api and coding practice. I'm also a fan of hockey, namely the Lightning & Rangers. The app is basically what the heading says: a collection of NHL teams, their rosters, and both team and player stats. So far, it's a steady work in progress...

NHL Stats API :

The application uses an NHL stats api that holds players and team stats that goes back for quite a few years. You can find current or prior season stats, career regular and post season stats, and endpoints for awards and draft picks just to mention a few.

Welcome to NHL Teams, Rosters & Stats.

Go over league, conference, and division stats, or choose a team from the slide-out menu above and view their details too.

After you choose a team, select a current rostered player from the dropdown that opens to view that player's stats, and optionally take a peak at their playing history too.

If you close a team's or player's information, you can use the slide-out menu and select them again.
