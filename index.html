Usage : - Daily games are available at the top. The dropdown of dates are all of upcoming and previous regular season games. Dropdowns of each game hold extended stats. View league, conference, and division stats as well.

- Select a team from above. You're then able to see that team's regular and preseason schedules. The dropdown of dates for that team has season stats dating back to their first year of play.

- You can then choose a current rostered player, and optionally take a peak at their playing history too. If you close a player's information, you can use the roster dropdown menu and select them again.

- Each game's card has a dropdown that contains period, intermission and power play times, along with shots and goals for each period. The Box Score buttons hide extra stats, the game's officials, and each team's game lineup.


Built By : - My name is Nate, I'm a Junior Web Developer of a little more than two years. I thought I would build this app out as a means of api and coding practice. I'm also a fan of hockey, namely the Lightning & Rangers.

NHL API : - The app is basically what the heading says : a collection of NHL teams, their schedules and rosters, team and player stats, and daily games with upcoming and previous dates. There's also endpoints for awards, draft picks and more, but I didn't get into those just yet.
