Built By :

My name is Nate, I'm a Junior Web Developer of a little more than two years. I thought I would build this app out as a meens of api and coding practice. I'm also a fan of hockey, namely the Lightning & Rangers. The app is basically what the heading says: a collection of NHL teams, their rosters, and both team and player stats. So far, it's a steady work in progress...

NHL Stats API :

The application uses an NHL stats api that holds players and team stats that goes back for quite a few years. You can find current or prior season stats, career regular and post season stats, and endpoints for awards and draft picks just to mention a few.

Welcome to NHL Teams, Stats & Things

Next day games are available below. The dropdown of dates are all of the regular season scheduled games. Dates are subject to change.

You can check out league, conference, and division stats, or choose a team from the slide-out menu above and view their details too.

After you choose a team, you're able to see that team's regular and preseason schedules. You can also choose a current rostered player, and optionally take a peak at their playing history too.

If you close a team's or player's information, you can use the slide-out menu and select them again to open their details.
